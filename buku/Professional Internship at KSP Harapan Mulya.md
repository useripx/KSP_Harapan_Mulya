# Professional Internship at KSP Harapan Mulya

### My internship experience at KSP Harapan Mulya was a transformative journey that significantly bridged the gap between my academic knowledge and professional application. During this period, I had the opportunity to contribute to a real-world project aimed at digitizing and enhancing the efficiency of a savings and loan cooperative. This experience not only sharpened my technical skills but also provided me with a profound understanding of how technology can solve complex financial challenges in a professional environment.

I conducted my internship at KSP Harapan Mulya, which is located at Campus 1 of Universitas Nusantara PGRI Kediri, for seven months from January to July 2026. My primary role was as a Full-stack Web Developer, where I was tasked with building and maintaining a comprehensive management system using a custom PHP Native MVC architecture. My responsibilities included developing core modules for loans, savings, and financial reporting, as well as ensuring that the system adhered to high standards of security and transparency.

On a daily basis, my activities focused on coding, database management, and system optimization. I spent most of my time writing clean PHP and JavaScript code to implement features such as dynamic loan interest rates and advanced member search functionalities using debouncing techniques. Additionally, I worked on integrating artificial intelligence into the system, specifically a credit scoring assistant named "Anna," to help automate the loan approval process. I also collaborated with the cooperative's staff to gather requirements and ensure the user interface was responsive and professional.

Beyond technical growth, this internship provided invaluable lessons in professional communication and teamwork. Working within the cooperative environment required me to interact regularly with various staff members to ensure the system met their operational needs. I learned how to translate complex technical concepts into understandable terms for my colleagues and had the opportunity to observe how to provide excellent service to the cooperative members (customers). These experiences significantly improved my ability to collaborate effectively in a diverse team and taught me the importance of maintaining professional ethics in a real-world work environment.

In conclusion, my internship at KSP Harapan Mulya was an incredibly rewarding experience that solidified my passion for financial technology. It taught me the importance of maintaining a balance between advanced features and robust security in any professional application. I am truly grateful for the opportunity to have contributed to the digital transformation of Koperasi Harapan Mulya, and I am confident that the skills I acquired will be invaluable in my future career as a developer.
